# FOUNDERS_MEMO_v1 (Pre-incorporation Alignment)

Date: 2026-02-27
Status: Internal working memo (non-binding draft until signed)

## 1) Equity (initial anchor)
- Initial split anchor: **30 / 30 / 30 / 10**
- This split is **subject to vesting** and may only be changed by **written amendment approved by all founders**.

## 2) Capital instrument (single choice)
- Parent/friend funding for this phase is treated as: **Founder/Parent Loan (interim)**
- No mixing with priced equity or SAFE in the same tranche without written amendment.

## 3) Control and approvals
- Spending authority:
  - <= threshold (to be set): operational lead may approve
  - > threshold: majority founder approval required
- Any new debt, equity issuance, IP transfer, or bank mandate change requires founder supermajority.

## 4) Roles (interim)
- Product & engineering owner
- Finance & reporting owner
- Growth/marketing owner
- Operations/counseling owner
- Role map must be explicitly assigned and reviewed monthly.

## 5) Reporting discipline
- Monthly P&L and cash runway snapshot mandatory.
- API burn and major vendor costs tracked weekly during pilot.

## 6) Scope/timing
- This memo governs the **pilot period** until incorporation package is finalized.
- Incorporation and formal legal documents will supersede this memo when executed.

## 7) Founder confirmation
- All founders confirm this memo reflects current operating intent.

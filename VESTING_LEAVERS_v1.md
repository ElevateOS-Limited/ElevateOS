# VESTING_LEAVERS_v1

Status: Draft founder policy for immediate alignment.

## Vesting baseline
- Duration: **4 years**
- Cliff: **12 months**
- Vesting frequency after cliff: monthly

## If a founder leaves early
- Before cliff: 0 vested shares (unless explicitly waived in writing)
- After cliff: vested portion retained, unvested portion subject to repurchase/return to pool

## Good leaver vs bad leaver (interim definitions)
- Good leaver: health, force majeure, mutually agreed role exit
- Bad leaver: misconduct, repeated non-performance after documented warning, confidentiality/IP breach
- Buyback pricing mechanics to be finalized with counsel

## IP continuity rule
- Founder departure does not revoke licensed/assigned company IP rights.
- Pre-existing IP compensation is contract-fixed and not renegotiated on exit.

## Transfer restrictions
- Right of first refusal for founder transfers
- Lock-up period to prevent early external transfer without consent

## Governance tie-in
- Final vesting and leaver terms must map into Shareholders Agreement + Founder Service/Contribution Agreements.

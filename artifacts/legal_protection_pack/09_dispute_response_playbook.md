# Dispute Response Playbook

## A) Co-founder IP ownership claim
- Evidence package: ledger, commit history, declaration, agreements.
- Response: request signed documentary basis.
- Legal posture: no transfer absent signed agreement. (JURISDICTION_REVIEW_REQUIRED)
- Technical containment: role/access/token review.

## B) Repo fork + competing launch
- Evidence package: chronology, authorship, release logs.
- Response: preserve evidence + counsel-reviewed notice.
- Legal posture: ownership/licensing assertion as applicable. (JURISDICTION_REVIEW_REQUIRED)
- Technical containment: infra hardening + brand separation.

## C) Equity entitlement claim
- Evidence package: signed-term absence/presence and chat context.
- Response: equity requires signed agreement.
- Legal posture: without-prejudice negotiation. (JURISDICTION_REVIEW_REQUIRED)
- Technical containment: preserve single-owner control.

## D) Public authorship dispute
- Evidence package: dated timeline + checksums.
- Response: short factual statement.
- Legal posture: rights reserved. (JURISDICTION_REVIEW_REQUIRED)
- Technical containment: monitor misrepresentation.

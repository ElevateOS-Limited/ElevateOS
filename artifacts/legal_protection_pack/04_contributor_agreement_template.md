# Contributor Agreement Template

> JURISDICTION_REVIEW_REQUIRED

## IP Assignment
Contributor assigns all rights in contributions to the designated project owner/entity in signed writing.

## Work-for-Hire
Where applicable by law, contributions are work-for-hire; otherwise assigned via IP Assignment section.

## Confidentiality
No disclosure of non-public project information.

## Equity Conditionality
Contribution alone grants no equity. Equity only via separate signed agreement.

## Termination
Access may be revoked; IP/confidentiality obligations survive.
